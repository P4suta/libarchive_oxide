Campaign 3 epic for completing mainstream archive method and metadata coverage.

## Outcomes

- ZIP adds Deflate64, bzip2, LZMA, zstd, AES, and major extra fields.
- 7z adds folders/coder graphs, solid archives, BCJ/Delta, Deflate, BZip2,
  Zstandard, PPMd, and AES.
- tar/cpio/ar/ISO gain producer-specific corpora and metadata round trips.
- CAB and XAR are read-only providers; RAR5 and UDF feasibility is resolved and
  implemented only within legal, spec-backed boundaries.

## Acceptance

- [ ] Every format/method reads output from at least three independent producers.
- [ ] Project output is accepted by at least two independent consumers.
- [ ] Encryption and metadata support are separate, explicit matrix columns.
- [ ] Malformed coder graphs, overlaps, checksums, auth failures, and truncation are fuzzed.
- [ ] Unsupported historical quirks return structured errors rather than heuristic guesses.
- [ ] RAR5/UDF scope and fixture provenance are recorded in ADRs.
